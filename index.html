Key Features

  • Acceleration Modeling: Considers velocity changes over time with damping factor
  • Ball Position Awareness: Players move toward ball with role-based weighting (70% for receivers, 50% for defense)
  • Trajectory Smoothing: Moving average filter reduces prediction noise
  • Physics Constraints: Enforces realistic speed limits and field boundaries
